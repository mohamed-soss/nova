Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with NovaSynk.

What is your typical response time?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly at +2 01111 78 5071 for immediate assistance.
Do you offer free consultations?
Yes, we offer a free 30-minute initial consultation for all new clients. This allows us to understand your needs and determine how we can best help you achieve your goals.
What information should I provide for a project estimate?
For the most accurate estimate, please provide: project goals, target audience, timeline, budget range, any existing materials or data, and specific requirements or constraints.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. We're experienced in remote collaboration and can accommodate different time zones and communication preferences.
What is your project management process?
Our process includes: discovery phase, planning and strategy, design/development, testing and refinement, delivery, and ongoing support. We provide regular updates and involve you at every stage.
