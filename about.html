About NovaSynk

Connecting Intelligence, Data, and Technology

NovaSynk is a research-driven technology company dedicated to helping organizations transform data into insight, strategy, and intelligent digital systems.

Our Origin

NovaSynk was founded with a simple but powerful belief: Data alone has no value unless it is understood, connected, and applied effectively.

Many organizations collect vast amounts of information yet struggle to convert it into real business impact. NovaSynk was created to close this gap by combining deep analytical thinking, research methodology, and advanced engineering under one unified approach.

Founded by Mohamed Mostafa and Ziad Mohamed, NovaSynk reflects a shared vision to build a company that delivers not only technology, but intelligence and strategic value.
