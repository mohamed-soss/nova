About NovaSynk

NovaSynk is a technology and research company founded to bridge the gap between data, intelligence, and digital innovation. We partner with startups, enterprises, and organizations to deliver scalable, future-ready solutions that solve real business challenges.

Founded by Mohamed Mostafa and Ziad Mohamed, NovaSynk combines deep analytical thinking with advanced engineering to help clients make confident, data-driven decisions.
